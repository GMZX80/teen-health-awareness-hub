Privacy

Privacy and Cookies

This site is designed as a static public guide and does not ask visitors to submit personal information.

Main guide Legal Accessibility

Current setup

No forms, accounts or intentional analytics

This site does not currently provide accounts, contact forms, comments, newsletters or interactive submissions to the site owner. It does not intentionally collect special category health information or information about children from visitors.

Personal data

What this site collects

The static pages on this site do not intentionally collect personal data from visitors. Some tools run in the visitor's browser, such as template or symptom-log tools; these are intended for local preparation by the visitor and should not be treated as a secure record system.

Do not enter confidential, identifying or urgent information into a public website unless you understand where that information is stored and who can access it.

Hosting

GitHub Pages and external links

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may process technical information such as IP addresses, browser details or access logs as part of providing the hosting service. External links lead to third-party websites, which have their own privacy and cookie practices.

Cookies

No intentional non-essential cookies

This site does not currently intentionally set advertising, profiling or analytics cookies. If analytics, embedded media, maps, forms or other third-party services are added later, this page should be reviewed and updated before those features go live.
