Privacy and safety

Local-only by design

This tool runs in your browser. It does not submit or store information on a server. If you download a PDF, treat it like a private health document.

Avoid over-monitoring if it increases anxiety. The aim is pattern spotting, not surveillance. Seek urgent help for immediate safety concerns or serious physical symptoms.
