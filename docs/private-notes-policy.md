# Private Notes Policy

This repository should remain generic. It can hold frameworks, source summaries, and blank templates, but should not become a diary of a young person's private health details.

## Keep Out Of The Repo

- Names and identifying details.
- School, class, staff, clinician, or peer names.
- Exact appointment dates and contact details.
- Screenshots, messages, medical letters, prescriptions, or referral documents.
- Details of self-harm methods, plans, or other sensitive risk information.

## Safer Alternatives

- Use anonymised labels such as "young person", "school", "GP", or "trusted adult".
- Store sensitive documents somewhere private and access-controlled outside GitHub.
- Use short pattern summaries, for example: "nausea often appears on school mornings and improves after lunch".
- Review before publishing anything through GitHub Pages.
