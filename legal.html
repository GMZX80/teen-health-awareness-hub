Disclaimer

Use live professional and official advice for decisions

The information on this site is for general awareness, preparation and signposting. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, therapy, safeguarding advice, education-law advice, legal advice, or a substitute for professional assessment.

Guidance, law, local policies, service names, referral routes and thresholds can change. Always check live official sources and seek advice from appropriate professionals before relying on information for a specific child, family or decision.

If a young person is at immediate risk of harm, has taken an overdose, is seriously injured, cannot stay safe, is missing, or there is immediate danger, use emergency, urgent health, crisis or safeguarding routes rather than this website.

Use of content

Plain-language summary only

The site paraphrases and organises public guidance to help families prepare questions and conversations. It does not create rights, duties, eligibility, referral decisions or professional recommendations for any individual case.

Where a decision matters, ask the relevant professional or organisation to put the decision, reasons, policy basis, evidence considered, review route and timescale in writing.
